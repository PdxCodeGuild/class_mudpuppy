Diana Deutsch

The Queen of Auditory Illusions

Diana Deutsch, the Queen of Auditory Illusions, is a British-American psychologist who continues to pioneer the field of music psychology. Deutsch has discovered several auditory illusions that provide interesting ways of demonstrating humans' auditory perception of speech and music. Below is a list of auditory illusions associated with her research. A full list of Deutsch's auditory illusions can be found on her website.

  • Speech-to-Song Illusion
  • Tritone Paradox
  • Octave Illusion
  • Cambiata Illusion
  • Mysterious Melody
  • Phantom Words
