Ermias Joseph Asghedom

(August 15, 1985 – March 31, 2019)

“Success to me is just being able to do what you love to do and support yourself all through.” - Nipsey Hussle

Place's Lived :

Ermias Joseph Ashgedom has lived in these 2 places:

  1. Los Angeles, California
  2. Hollywood, California (His final resting place)

Ermais spent most of his life in Los Angeles, California until his final resting place in Forest Lawn Park, Hollywood California. Ermais was born to Angelique Smith, a woman of African American descent and Dawit Asghedom, an Eritrean Immigrant. He was raised on Crenshaw street and was a pillar of his community. His death saddened the entire city.
